Scientific Activities

Currently, I am a member of the University of Exeter's Public Engagement Strategic Advisory Group and am the University's representative for the Grimm Network. In 2013, I co-founded the series of graduate schools on dynamical systems at Universität Bremen, and in 2006, I co‑founded the Pure Postgraduate Seminar at the University of St Andrews.

Below is a selection of scientific meetings, public engagement activities, and seminars series I have organised and co‑organised.
