About

My name is Tony Samuel. I am an Associate Professor of Mathematics and Head of the Topology and Dynamics Research Group at the University of Birmingham. Earlier this year, I joined the advisory board of the Academy for the Mathematical Sciences.

Prior to joining the University of Birmingham, I held academic posts at California Polytechnic State University, Universität Bremen, Australian National University, and the University of St Andrews. I have also had the privilege of holding prolonged research stays at the Hausdorff Research Institute for Mathematics, Humboldt–Universität zu Berlin, Institut Mittag–Leffler, and South China University of Technology. For details on my academic ancestry please visit the Mathematics Genealogy Project.

For contact information please see my University of Birmingham staff profile.
